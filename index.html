Best Foundations

Options for all concerns

Oily Skin: Nars Soft Matte Foundation

This foundation gives you a natural glow, while also mattifying your face and preventing natural oils from peaking through. We love that this foundations lasts the whole day and is made up of ingredients that will not clog your pores.

  • Available at: Ulta or Sephora
  • Cost: $42

Dry Skin: Dior Forever Skin GLow

Dior provides a foundations that blends out flawlessly while feeling like skin. The formula blurs your pores and gives a natural glow to the skin. This is perfect is you are looking to have a dew base for your makeup and need some extra hydration.

  • Available at: Ulta or Sephora
  • Cost: $54

Combination Skin: Givenchy Prisme Libre Skin-Caring Matte Foundation

This foundation provides a natural glow while also having a matte formula to ensure long wear withouth greasiness. If you are looking for even more of a dewy look, there is also a glowy version of the prisme libre foundation. This foundations is blurring and provives buildable, skinlike converage.

  • Available at: Sephora
  • Cost: $49

Best Affordable Option: L'Oreal Infallible Fresh Wear

This foundatinon is lightweight and buildable with a natural finish. It is long lasting and does not transfer. This is an excellent choice for those who are not trying to break the bank.

  • Available at: Ulta and Target
  • Cost: $19.99
